About

Launched in 2017, roomauction.co.uk is the simple way to search, bid and rent short to long term accommodation. We aim to provide a unique and effective platform which provides an exceptional property service for all potential agents, landlords, tenants, lodgers and guests.

Our services are available on our website and also via Android and Apple mobile applications. We hope that when you use RoomAuction.co.uk, you'll enjoy our fresh, clean and focused approach.

Letting agents and landlords have moved to roomauction.co.uk from other sites and many are advertising their new properties with us as we provide a platform and service with many benefits.

So whether you're looking let or rent, we're in business to improve your experience.
